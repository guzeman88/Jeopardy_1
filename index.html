How to Play

The Board

Six categories with five clues each, valued $200–$1,000. Tap any clue to play it. Harder clues are worth more.

Reading the Clue

Read the clue aloud. When you're ready, tap Show Answer or wait for the timer to run out. Remember — in Jeopardy! you must respond in the form of a question!

Scoring

Tap ✔ if you got it right to add the dollar value to your score. Tap ✗ if you missed it to deduct the same amount. Scores can go negative!

Daily Double

Two clues on the board are hidden Daily Doubles. When you find one, place a wager before seeing the clue. You can wager up to your entire score (minimum $5).

Final Jeopardy

After all 30 clues, it's Final Jeopardy! Everyone wagers some or all of their score, then sees one final clue. The highest final score wins!

Multiplayer

Up to 4 players share one device. After each clue, every player marks correct or wrong independently. Perfect for game nights!
